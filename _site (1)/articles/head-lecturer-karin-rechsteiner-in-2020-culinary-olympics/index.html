• Head Lecturer Karin Rechsteiner in 2020 Culinary Olympics
  • head-lecturer-karin-rechsteiner-in-2020-culinary-olympics
  • Le Cordon Bleu Melbourne is proud to announce that Head Pâtisserie Lecturer, Karin Rechsteiner has been selected as Pastry Chef for the Australian team in the 2020 Culinary Olympics in Stuttgart. This prestigious event is held every four years in Germany and attracts over 5,000 of the world’s top chefs.
